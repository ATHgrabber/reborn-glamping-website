Concept

CONCEPT

Return to mountain, fire, and water.
A wellness experience that frees body and mind amidst the rich nature of Yatsugatake.

At 1,100 meters above sea level. Crystal-clear air, the ridgeline of the Yatsugatake range,
four private cabins nestled in a landscape overlooking the Southern Alps.

Immerse yourself deeply in a Finnish barrel sauna,
awaken your body in a natural spring water cold plunge,
and surrender to the mountain breeze during open-air rest.

Sauna, nature, silence —
a wellness glamping experience where everything is in harmony.

♨

Sauna × Wellness

Every cabin features an authentic Finnish barrel sauna, the totonou 3.0m Pro. Craft your own sauna experience with self-loyly. Achieve the ultimate state of wellness with natural spring water cold plunges and open-air rest overlooking Yatsugatake.

🌿

Nature × Renewal

Pristine air at 1,100m elevation, a canopy of stars, birdsong at dawn. Embraced by the ever-changing beauty of Yatsugatake through all four seasons, leave the city behind and rediscover your true self.

✦

Privacy × Luxury

Four freestanding cabins offer complete seclusion. Enjoy BBQ on your private deck, sauna, and bonfire without a care. Create the finest moments with your loved ones.

Private Finnish Sauna

SORA SAUNA

— A Private Sauna Connected to the Sky —

Beneath the Yatsugatake sky stands a barrel sauna from Finland — the totonou 3.0m Pro. Its beautiful barrel form and the fragrance of natural wood create an exquisite sanctuary. Control the steam with self-loyly, invigorate your body in a natural spring water cold plunge, and rest on infinity chairs gazing at the majestic mountains — a sense of total wellness that melts into the sky, found only here.

🔥 Self-Loyly — Create your ideal steam with birch aroma water. Full control over temperature and humidity.
💧 Natural Spring Cold Plunge — Fed by Yatsugatake's underground spring water at approximately 14°C for a deeply restorative chill.
🌄 Open-Air Rest at Yatsugatake — Crystal-clear air at 1,100m. Become one with the sky on infinity chairs.

Access Information

🚗 By Car

Approx. 15 min from Nagasaka IC on the Chuo Expressway
Approx. 15 min from Kobuchizawa IC on the Chuo Expressway
*Free parking available (1 space per cabin)

🚃 By Train

Approx. 15 min by taxi from JR Kobuchizawa Station (Chuo Main Line)
Approx. 8 min by taxi from JR Kai-Oizumi Station (Koumi Line)
*Approx. 2 hours from Shinjuku via Limited Express Azusa

🚌 By Highway Bus

Shinjuku Expressway Bus Terminal → Nagasaka-Takane (approx. 2.5 hours)
*Shuttle from bus stop available upon request

💡 Check-in 15:00–18:00 / Check-out by 11:00
Please contact us in advance if you will arrive after 18:00.
